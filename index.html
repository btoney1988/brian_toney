About Me

Hello, my name is Brian Toney. I was born and raised in Bayville, New York. I have a Bachelors degree in Tourism Management from the Suny College at Brockport. For the past few years I have been traveling the country with my wife, Kate, and dog, Domino. I have lived in Hawaii, California, Colorado, and now we are in Maryland.

I have a strong passion for competitive sports and gaming. I am currently enrolled in a coding bootcamp in order to create a better lifestyle for my family. I am also hoping that I can make my way somehow into the gaming industry. The pandemic has made me realize how important a stable and reliable job can be. Coming from a sales background I also enjoy talking to people, I am usually the guy in line that starts a conversation with complete strangers around me.

My hobbies are hanging out with my family, gaming and sports. The genre of games that I enjoy are first person shooters, survival games, and battle royale games. The sports I enjoy are baseball, football, lacrosse, soccer and golf. I also enjoy going on hikes/walking with Kate and Domino. Traveling all over the country has helped me see the beauty of nature and being outside.
